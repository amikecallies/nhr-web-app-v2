New Home Realty



OUR GOAL

New Home Realty has been in business for over 25 years and is one of the fastest growing real estate companies in San Antonio. Arlington R. Callies is the owner of the company and his wife, Pamela Callies is the broker, working with some amazing agents seeking to provide the best in class service to their clients. In an effort to accommodate the growth, Arlington retired from USAA insurance company after twenty-three years of service as a casualty claims bodily injury adjuster. His years of service at USAA has enabled him to appreciate the service provided by our military community and has transferred that same level of service to his clients. New Home Realty will continue their pursuit of excellence in providing professional service and exceeding their clients expectations.
